About

University. 2009-2013

I studied translation at the Universidad Intercontinental. Even though I ended up not working as a translator, I have no regrets. I learned how to read, write and learn like a pro, which I think are my most valuable skills.

Triode. 2014-2015

Nostromo. 2015-2016

Present day.

After two companies, I'm hooked on business. I now realize there are important skills and knowledge I need to develop, so I've become a full-time programming and business student.
